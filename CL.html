How to Get Here

If you get off Interstate 95 in Connecticut at Ext 5 (both from the North of the South) you come to a light at the MacDonalds. Take a right on to Route 1 and go for 1 mile. At a stop light turn Left onto Havemeyer Lane. Go three streets and turn left onto Halsey. Follow Halsey down through a stop sign until to see 65 Halsey Drive on the Right.
