Case Study: Muzzy Testimonial Website

 

The above video portal for Early Advantage demonstrates the power of Web Flicks to increase sales. The
Early Advantage site allows customers, teachers and language experts to talk about successes with prospective customers. Early Advantage hired us to interview customers and language experts such as Dr. Rassias from Dartmouth. We then edited all the videos down to short Web Flicks, allowing Early Advantage to see a 10% increase in sales the first week the site was up. Every brand can benefit from Web Flicks that allow customers to talk to prospects.
