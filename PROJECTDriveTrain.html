Project: Drive Train Music Video

Music videos are a great way to establish a new brand. Shot on a tiny budget, we did many of the effects for this music video in-camera. For one sequence, Drive Train performers stood in the back of a moving truck going through the Holland Tunnel. The two stills above show (1) a water effect created by reflective Mylar and (2) filming with available emergency lighting which we pulled off by undercranking the camera and using a special camera stabilizer that we developed called the Fatigue Cam.
